Welcome to THEREMAX, the touchscreen synthesizer!

Press the spacebar to switch between this screen and the THEREMAX interface.

Touch or click the grid to play a tone and render a corresponding waveform. The x-axis controls pitch, and the y-axis controls volume.

Drag the control knobs below onto the grid to set lingering notes.

If you have a touchscreen, you can play multiple notes at once by touching the screen in multiple places.
